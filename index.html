Honkai: Star Rail

Embark on a Journey Beyond the Stars

Honkai: Star Rail is a cosmic adventure that follows the Trailblazer, a character who awakens with a "Stellaron" implanted in their body and no memories. They join the crew of the Astral Express and journey across the cosmos, helping to resolve crises on various planets caused by the disastrous Stellarons. The narrative is driven by the Trailblazer's growing connections with new and old companions and the mystery of their own origins.

Featured Characters

Dan Heng

The cold and reserved train guard and archivist of the Astral Express.

Kafka

A member of the Stellaron Hunters who orchestrated the Trailblazer's awakening.

Aventurine

A senior manager in the IPC Strategic Investment Department and one of the Ten Stonehearts.

Jing Yuan

A powerful general of the Xianzhou Alliance's Cloud Knights, known for his strategic mind and the Lightning Lord summon.
